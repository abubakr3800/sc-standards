================================================================================
EVALUATION PARAMETERS
================================================================================

SUPPORTED PARAMETERS:
1. ILLUMINANCE
   - Minimum values: 50-300 lux
   - Recommended values: 100-1000 lux
   - Maximum values: 200-2000 lux
   - Applications: Office, conference, corridor, reception, etc.

2. UGR (UNIFIED GLARE RATING)
   - Office work: ≤19 (recommended ≤16)
   - Conference rooms: ≤22 (recommended ≤19)
   - Corridors: ≤25 (recommended ≤22)
   - Computer work: ≤16 (recommended ≤13)

3. CRI (COLOR RENDERING INDEX)
   - Minimum: 80
   - Recommended: 90
   - Color-critical tasks: ≥95
   - General applications: ≥80

4. UNIFORMITY
   - Office work: ≥0.6 (recommended ≥0.8)
   - Conference rooms: ≥0.6 (recommended ≥0.8)
   - Corridors: ≥0.4 (recommended ≥0.6)
   - Detailed work: ≥0.7 (recommended ≥0.9)

5. POWER DENSITY
   - Office: ≤3.5 W/m² (recommended ≤2.5 W/m²)
   - Conference: ≤3.5 W/m² (recommended ≤2.5 W/m²)
   - Corridor: ≤2.0 W/m² (recommended ≤1.5 W/m²)
   - Detailed work: ≤5.0 W/m² (recommended ≤4.0 W/m²)

================================================================================
EVALUATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

SCORING SYSTEM:
1. EXCELLENT (90-100 points)
   - All parameters exceed recommendations
   - Optimal lighting design
   - Best practice implementation

2. GOOD (80-89 points)
   - Meets all requirements
   - Good lighting quality
   - Minor improvements possible

3. ACCEPTABLE (70-79 points)
   - Meets minimum requirements
   - Adequate lighting
   - Some improvements needed

4. POOR (60-69 points)
   - Below recommendations
   - Poor lighting quality
   - Significant improvements needed

5. FAIL (0-59 points)
   - Below minimum requirements
   - Unacceptable lighting
   - Major redesign required
